Load Movie

The Load Movie feature of the Ez-Viz interface allows you to open a movie either for playback or editing.  Prior to loading a movie you must load the PDB file that corresponds to the movie you want to load.  After loading the moelcule you can then click the Load Movie button which will display a window for choosing the movie file.  Once the movie is loaded you can either play the movie by pressing the Play button on the movies tab or extend the movie by appending frames and editing them.
