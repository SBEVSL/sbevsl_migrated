The Information Tab

Another very useful aspect of EZ-Viz lies in the information tab.  It is here that the user is able to find information relating to their molecule of choice.  The name, category, sequence, hetero atom information and primary citation for each molecule appear here; all of which are generated from the information contained in the PDB file.  This parsing occurs each time that a user loads a new PDB file, whether it be through the PyMOL interface or the EZ-Viz interface.
