The Preset Movies

This features some general movies that can be created with almost any molecule that you bring into PyMOL.  There is also a control panel that allows you to control the playback of the movie after its been created.  To create a movie in EZ-Viz, simply click the button with the name of the movie you want to create and wait until you're molecule reappears in the viewing window.  When it does, just grab a bag of popcorn, hit play and enjoy!
